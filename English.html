How to Make a Payment

Making a payment in this system is simple and secure. To pay Naira 2000:

  • Enter the name you will use to transfer the money along with your account number.
  • Click Submit.
  • You will be shown the NG Admin's bank account number to which you will transfer the money.
  • After making the transfer, go back to the Register page.
  • Our system will automatically verify your payment, and you will be prompted to enter the OTP code sent to your email. After that, click, if you get some problem to payment contact NG Admin Submit.

Important Things to Note:

  • Make sure you provide a valid email address that you actively use.
  • Ensure that your bank name and account number are correct.
  • Our system will store your details for 30 minutes. If the payment is not confirmed within this time, your details will be deleted for security purposes.

How to Register

After making the payment, you will be able to Register:

  • Fill in your details correctly.
  • Set a password that you can remember.
  • Verify your details and then click Submit.
  • You will access a dashboard that shows your name along with a welcome bonus of Naira 500.

How to Withdraw Money

You can withdraw your money if it reaches Naira 500 via OPay or Naira 1000 via bank transfer:

  • Make sure you enter your bank details correctly.
  • After initiating the withdrawal, the payment status will be pending until Admin confirms the payment.
  • You will receive confirmation from your dashboard that the money has been withdrawn, and you will see it in your account. If you are not see your payment after 24 hours contact NG Admin
